Workplace Accident Form (Injury)
This form is to report accidents in the workplace that resulted in an injury/caused damage to the individual.
Workplace Accident Form (Death)
This form is to report accidents in the workplace that resulted to the loss of life of the employee.
OSH Standard Violation Form
This form is to report workplace environments violating OSH standards (No OSH committee, No safety officer, Makes employees pay for PPE, No on-duty doctor/nurse, No clinic, etc.)
COVID-19 Health Protocol Violation Form
This form is to report Cases that are COVID-positive or the non-implementation of minimum health protocols of the companies.
Worker Non Compensation Form*
This form is to report cases where a worker that has tested positive (COVID) was not able receive compensation from the Employees' Compensation Commission.

About Us

The Institute for Occupational Health and Safety Development (IOHSAD) is a non-stock, non-profit and independent organization engaged in assisting Filipino workers develop comprehensive and self-reliant occupational health and safety (OHS) programs. IOHSAD works with trade unions, church and community organizations in initiating programs for the health and welfare of workers and their families.
